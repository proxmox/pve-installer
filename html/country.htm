Location and Time Zone selection

The itPVE Installer automatically makes location-based optimizations, like choosing the nearest mirror to download files from. Also make sure to select the correct time zone and keyboard layout.

Press the Next button to continue the installation.


Country: The selected country is used to choose nearby mirror servers. This will speed up downloads and make updates more reliable.
